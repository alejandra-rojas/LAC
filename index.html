Invitation to the "The planet is your canvas" art contest for Latin America and the Caribbean 2022-2023

This initiative expects to make visible the creative people in the region, who are engaged in different disciplines like photography, drawing and painting, digital illustration and video, in order to raise awareness on the activities related to the Montreal Protocol and Kigali Amendment.

The contest will be open for people residing in Latin America and the Caribbean from October 17, 2022, until January 16, 2023, and we would be very pleased if you could disseminate this call among your partners, schools, schools of art, universities and other stakeholders.

The contest rules and the web page will be announced on the day of the official launching on October 17, 2022. The winners will be invited to a ceremony organized by the United Nations Environment Program in Panama.
