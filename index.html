Invitation to the art contest for Latin America and the Caribbean 2022-2023

In OzonAction we want to promote the contribution of the Montreal Protocol and the Kigali Amendment to fight the accelerated climate change, as well as to make visible and promote creative people from Latin America and the Caribbean, who work in areas such as photography, drawing and painting, digital illustration, and videos.

About

This first edition will focus on highlighting the importance of the Montreal Protocol in reducing ozone-depleting substances and the impact of the Kigali Amendment on the reduction of greenhouse gases, such as hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs).

Stages

Phase 1: Reception of artworks (October 17, 2022 to January 16, 2023). Prepare your artwork and submit it using the application form. Take into account the information resources and remember to fill out all the fields. Phase 2: Pre-selection of candidates (January 16 to January 23, 2023). The OzonAction Evaluation Committee will review the requirements and the artworks received. Phase 3: Jury vote (January 23 to January 27, 2023). The jury, composed of artists and members of the United Nations System, will select 20 pieces of artwork; 5 per category. The jury vote will have a weight of 80%. Phase 4: Public Online Voting (January 30 to February 10, 2023). Everyone in Latin America and the Caribbean will be able to vote for her/his favorite artwork. The public vote will have a weight of 20%. Phase 5: Announcement of winning artworks (February 13, 2023). The four winners will be announced in February and the award ceremony will be held in Panama City, Panama.
Winners of each category will be invited to the meeting of the Latin American and Caribbean networks, to be held in March 2023, where they will have the opportunity to exhibit their works of art to the National Ozone Officers of each country in the region.

Rules

If you are a national or reside in a country in Latin America and the Caribbean and you consider that you have talent in any of the following areas: photography, drawing and painting, digital illustration and videos, this contest is for you.

Please, consider this information The content of the artworks must reflect an important message related to the contest theme (Montreal Protocol and Kigali Amendment). Any artwork that is not original or has been submitted to another art contest will be rejected. Check for more details in our terms and conditions section.
